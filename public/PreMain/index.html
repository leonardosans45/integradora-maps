About Us

We are an organization dedicated to providing information and resources for new members of the Universidad Tecnológica de Chihuahua BIS Model.

Our mission is to facilitate the orientation and adaptation of new students within the university campus through an interactive and friendly mapping system.

Main Features:

  • Detailed mapping of facilities
  • Updated location information
  • Intuitive and easy-to-use interface
  • Multilingual support
